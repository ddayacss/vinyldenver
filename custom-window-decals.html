Frequently Asked Questions

Can window decals be used on vehicles?

Yes, window decals can be used on vehicles. They are commonly used for car window graphics and advertising.

Can window decals be used on textured or frosted glass?

Window decals can be used on textured or frosted glass, but the adhesion may not be as strong as on smooth glass surfaces.

Can window decals be removed without damaging the window?

Yes, most window decals can be removed without damaging the window. They can be peeled off easily, and any residue can be cleaned off with soap and water.
