Frequently Asked Questions

Are window decals safe for children and pets?

Window decals are generally safe for children and pets. However, it is important to ensure that they are applied in a safe manner and are not within reach of young children or pets who may try to peel them off.

How do window decals work?

Window decals work by using an adhesive backing that sticks to the window surface, allowing the decal to stay in place.

How do I clean window decals?

Window decals can be cleaned with soap and water. Gently scrub the decal with a soft cloth or sponge, and avoid using abrasive cleaners or tools.

What are window decals?

Window decals are adhesive stickers or graphics that are applied to windows for decorative or promotional purposes.
