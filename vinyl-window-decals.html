Frequently Asked Questions

Can window decals be removed without damaging the window?

Yes, most window decals can be removed without damaging the window. They can be peeled off easily, and any residue can be cleaned off with soap and water.

Can window decals be used on vehicles?

Yes, window decals can be used on vehicles. They are commonly used for car window graphics and advertising.

Are window decals easy to apply?

Yes, window decals are generally easy to apply. They usually come with instructions and can be applied by simply peeling off the backing and sticking them onto the window.

What are window decals?

Window decals are adhesive stickers or graphics that are applied to windows for decorative or promotional purposes.
