Frequently Asked Questions

Can window decals be removed without damaging the window?

Yes, most window decals can be removed without damaging the window. They can be peeled off easily, and any residue can be cleaned off with soap and water.

How do window decals work?

Window decals work by using an adhesive backing that sticks to the window surface, allowing the decal to stay in place.

Can window decals be used on textured or frosted glass?

Window decals can be used on textured or frosted glass, but the adhesion may not be as strong as on smooth glass surfaces.

Are window decals safe for children and pets?

Window decals are generally safe for children and pets. However, it is important to ensure that they are applied in a safe manner and are not within reach of young children or pets who may try to peel them off.
